Traditional Ranch Chilaquiles

Home

Crispy chilaquiles covered in a rich red tomato chile sauce, finished with fresh epazote, crumbled cheese, and a drizzle of crema. Served with a side of creamy refried beans and perfectly fried eggs, its a comforting, classic Mexican breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • 4 to 5 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 dried chile Xalape√±o
  • 1/4 of an onion
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh epazote leaves
  • Crumbled cheese
  • Cream
  • Eggs to taste
  • Refried beans to serve

How to cook

  1. Make the sauce. Blend tomatoes, soaked dried chiles, onion, garlic, and salt; simmer 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Add tortilla chips to the sauce and toss gently until coated. Add fresh epazote.
  3. Heat refried beans and fry the eggs to your liking.
  4. Plate the chilaquiles and top with crumbled cheese, cream and a few leaves of epazote. Add beans and fried eggs on the side.
