Bean Cream Soup

Home

A smooth and comforting bean cream blended with cheese and a touch of cream. Finished with crispy fried tortilla strips that add the perfect bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked beans or a can of fried beans
  • 1 cup of chicken broath
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 spoon of butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cheese ready to serve
  • Cream for topping
  • Crispy fried tortilla stripes
  • Chopped cilantro

How to cook

  1. Blend Everything.Add the beans with the can of condensed milk and blend.
  2. In a pot, boil the chicken broth and add the spoon of butter.
  3. Season with salt while stir slowly until it gets creamy.
  4. Serve. Top with cheese, cream, the crispy tortilla strips and chopped cilantro.
